Tiramisu Recipe!

Ingredients:

  • Ladyfingers
  • Mascarpone
  • Coffee
  • Heavy Whipped Cream
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cocoa Powder

Cooking Instructions
